Training data used in:

"Conversion of Artificial Recurrent Neural Networks to Spiking Neural Networks for Low-power Neuromorphic Hardware"
Peter U. Diehl, Guido Zarrella, Andrew Cassidy, Bruno U. Pedroni, and Emre Neftci

From:

https://cogcomp.seas.upenn.edu/Data/QA/QC/

Description:

This data collection contains all the data used in our learning question classification experiments(see [1]), 
which has question class definitions, the training and testing question sets, examples of preprocessing the questions, 
feature definition scripts and examples of semantically related word features. This work has been done by Xin Li and Dan Roth and supported by [2].
[1] Xin Li, Dan Roth, Learning Question Classifiers. COLING'02, Aug., 2002.
[2] Research supported by NSF grants IIS-9801638 and ITR IIS-0085836 and an ONR MURI Award.
